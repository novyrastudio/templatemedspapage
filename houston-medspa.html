FAQ

Everything you need to know

Can't find your answer? Call us or book a free consultation.

How long do Botox results last? +
Botox typically lasts 3 to 6 months depending on the treatment area, muscle activity, and individual metabolism. First-time clients often find results last closer to 3 months; with regular treatments, the duration tends to extend.
Is there recovery time after filler injections? +
Most clients experience minimal redness or swelling that resolves within 24–48 hours. Bruising is possible but uncommon. We recommend avoiding strenuous exercise and alcohol for 24 hours post-treatment.
Are treatments safe for all skin types? +
Yes. Our medical-grade protocols are designed and adjusted for all skin types and tones. During your consultation, your specialist conducts a full skin assessment to determine the safest and most effective approach for you.
How much do treatments typically cost? +
Pricing varies by treatment type and product amount. Botox starts from $12 per unit. Fillers begin at $650 per syringe. Your consultation is completely free, and we provide a clear, itemized estimate before any treatment begins.
What happens during my free consultation? +
Your consultation lasts approximately 30–45 minutes. A licensed specialist reviews your medical history, discusses your aesthetic goals, performs a facial assessment, and creates a personalized treatment plan — with full pricing and no obligation to proceed.
Will my results look natural? +
Natural results are our standard, not our exception. Our specialists are trained in advanced injection techniques and facial anatomy to ensure enhancements complement your unique structure. The goal is always refreshed, never overdone.
